Browser game

Tetris

Stack pieces, clear lines, and stress-test your focus. A minimal classic you can play in the browser — no install, just reflexes.

What this is

This is a lightweight Tetris-style game: falling tetrominoes, line clears, and rising score. Open the play screen and the first piece drops right away — perfect for a short break or a quick challenge.

How to play

  • Move and rotate pieces to form complete horizontal rows.
  • Cleared rows vanish and add to your score and line count.
  • The stack reaches the top — game over. Refresh on the play page to try again.

Controls

← / → move · ↓ soft drop · ↑ rotate
